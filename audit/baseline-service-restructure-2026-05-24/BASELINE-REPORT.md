## Key Findings

1. **EN digital service pages** (System Integration, IoT, AI, Data Analytics, SaaS, Digital Consulting) average ~621 words — significantly higher than the ~80 words noted in original audit. This suggests content was added since initial audit.

3. **ZH pages are critically thin** — averaging only ~262 words vs ~740 for EN/ID. This is the biggest content gap.
4. **ID pages** closely mirror EN word counts — good translation coverage.
