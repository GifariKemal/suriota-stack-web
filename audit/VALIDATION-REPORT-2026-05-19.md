## New Findings Kimi Missed

1. **AIOSEO auto-generates descriptions from raw widget HTML** — that's why descriptions look weird ("SaaS · Energy Monitoring99.9% Uptime SLA"). The fix is not "trim to 160 chars" — it's **write manual AIOSEO descriptions per page**.
2. **Zero-width-space (U+200B) in 3 hardware titles** — copy-paste artifact. Will display as invisible character in SERP titles; may also break exact-match anchor logic.
3. **Title typo "Waste Water Loger"** — missing the second `g`. Slug was fixed to `logger` but `<title>` tag was not.
4. **Slug-mismatch ghosts** — Kimi's 2 "404s" for Modbus and Waste Water Logger come from referencing dead slugs. The real product pages are live and indexable. Need 301 redirects from the old slugs.
5. **Architectural split between page templates** — 11 pages (legacy template) carry the `entry-title` H1 + FAQ schema; 11 pages (new template) have neither. Single template-level fix on the 11 legacy pages cleans both H1 and the duplicate-content issue without losing schema.
6. **3 SURGE SaaS pages have no Product/SoftwareApplication schema** — Kimi flagged the wrong category. Add `SoftwareApplication` schema (more accurate than `Product` for SaaS).

## Design Critique Summary (using design-critique framework)

Sampled 3 representative pages (Homepage, Automation, SURGE-Energy Mapping) against the `suriota_design_system_sx.md` Industrial Editorial token system.

### Overall Impression
The Industrial Editorial system (Poppins/Lato or Plus Jakarta Sans/IBM Plex Mono, teal `#205B69` + amber `#C8851F`) is well-defined in memory but **only partially applied**. Elementor Global Kit still ships default `#6EC1E4` (light blue) and `#61CE70` (green) for "Primary"/"Accent" — anyone editing in Elementor will pick the wrong palette by accident.

### Consistency Issues (Cross-Page)

| Element | Issue | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Header background | `#3C7D47` (green) in v5 snippet vs `#205B69` (teal) in design system | Align to design token; teal communicates "industrial trust" better |
| Dropdown panel | Now `#0E3942` (good — matches system) | Keep |
| Footer | `#0F5132` (forest green) | Either commit to green brand or switch to teal `#1B4D5A` — currently inconsistent |
| Accent color | Amber `#F59E0B` in v5 vs `#C8851F` in design system | Standardize on one amber value |
| Title typography | Plus Jakarta Sans on header/footer, Poppins on content | Pick one heading font sitewide |

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Eye flow on hero sections:** competing H1s (theme `entry-title` + Elementor) split attention on 11 pages.
- **Service-page cards** rely on default Elementor "Icon Box" with no consistent iconography.
- **Product pages** lead with logo+model number, not value proposition — works for B2B technical buyer, but no human-readable hook for first-time visitor.

### Accessibility
- **Touch targets:** dropdown links in mobile menu now 11×18 padding (≈44px height) — OK after v5
- **Color contrast:** `#FBBF24` amber on `#3C7D47` green = 3.8:1 — fails WCAG AA for body, passes for large text. Borderline.
- **Image alt:** SURGE pages missing alt on 1 decorative image each (low priority).

### What Works
- AIOSEO schema coverage is genuinely good (only 3 pages missing Product/SoftwareApplication).
- v5 emergency header is mobile-correct now: tap-to-toggle works, tap-outside-closes, sibling dropdowns auto-close.
- Hardware product pages share a clean uniform template — easy to fix metadata in bulk.

### Priority Design Fixes
1. **Align Elementor Global Kit colors to design system** — single source of truth for all future widgets.
2. **Resolve teal-vs-green identity question** — pick one for header+footer or commit to a 2-color brand zone scheme intentionally.
3. **Standardize hero pattern** across the 4 cluster types (company / service / product / SURGE) — currently each is bespoke.
