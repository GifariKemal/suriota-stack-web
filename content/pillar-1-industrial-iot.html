Core Capabilities

Industrial IoT Deployment

Industrial IoT is not consumer IoT with tougher labels. It demands robust hardware, deterministic communication, cybersecurity hardening, and 24/7 reliability in harsh environments. SURIOTA's IoT deployment services cover the full stack:

  • Edge Gateway Design & Deployment: Our flagship SRT-MGATE-1210 Modbus RTU/TCP to MQTT gateway converts legacy serial data into cloud-ready JSON payloads. DIN-rail mountable, -40°C to +85°C rated, and production-proven across 30+ sites.
  • Sensor Integration: Temperature, vibration, pressure, flow, level, and custom sensor integration with 4-20mA, RS-485, and wireless (LoRaWAN, WiFi, 4G) interfaces.
  • Cloud Dashboard Development: Real-time dashboards on AWS IoT Core, Azure IoT, or private cloud with alarm management, historical trending, and mobile access.
  • Edge Computing: Local data preprocessing, anomaly filtering, and protocol translation at the edge to reduce bandwidth and cloud costs.

System Integration

True digital transformation requires more than connecting devices — it requires connecting systems. SURIOTA integrates OT (Operational Technology) and IT (Information Technology) layers that traditionally operate in isolation:

  • SCADA Integration: Connect Wonderware, Ignition, WinCC, or custom SCADA systems to higher-level MES and ERP platforms.
  • MES & ERP Connectivity: Bi-directional data flow between shop-floor machines and SAP, Oracle, or custom ERP systems for real-time production planning.
  • OT/IT Convergence: Secure network architecture design that bridges plant-floor networks ( Purdue Level 0-2 ) with enterprise networks (Level 3-4) without compromising safety or cybersecurity.
  • Protocol Translation: Modbus RTU/TCP, OPC UA, BACnet, MQTT, Profinet, EtherNet/IP — we handle multi-protocol environments where devices speak different languages.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical IoT deployment take?

Single-site pilot projects typically take 4-8 weeks. Multi-site enterprise rollouts range from 3-6 months depending on scale and integration complexity. We always start with a pilot to validate architecture before scaling.

Can you integrate with our existing SCADA system?

Yes. We have experience integrating with Wonderware System Platform, Ignition by Inductive Automation, Siemens WinCC, Schneider Vijeo Citect, and custom-built SCADA systems. Integration can be via OPC UA, Modbus, database connectors, or REST APIs.

What about cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is embedded in every layer of our design: encrypted communications (TLS 1.3), certificate-based device authentication, network segmentation (DMZ for IoT), VPN tunnels for remote access, and regular security audits. We follow IEC 62443 guidelines for industrial cybersecurity.

Do you support on-premise deployment?

Absolutely. While cloud offers scalability and lower CapEx, many industrial clients prefer on-premise for data sovereignty, latency requirements, or air-gapped networks. We deploy private cloud stacks (Kubernetes + InfluxDB + Grafana) on customer infrastructure.

What is the SRT-MGATE-1210?

The SRT-MGATE-1210 is SURIOTA's in-house Modbus RTU/TCP to MQTT gateway. It converts legacy Modbus data into JSON format and publishes to AWS IoT Core, Azure IoT, or private MQTT brokers. DIN-rail mountable, industrial temperature rated, and priced for volume deployment.

Which regions do you serve?

While our headquarters and engineering team are in Batam, we serve clients across Indonesia (Batam, Bintan, Jakarta, Surabaya, Medan, Riau) and Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam). Our team is available for on-site deployment and support throughout the region.
