Consulting Services

Digital Maturity Assessment

Before prescribing solutions, we diagnose your current state. Our Digital Maturity Assessment evaluates 6 dimensions of industrial digitalization:

  • Connectivity: What percentage of your assets are connected? What protocols do they use? Where are the gaps?
  • Data Management: How is data collected, stored, and accessed? Is there a unified data architecture?
  • Analytics & AI: What analytics capabilities exist? Are decisions data-driven or experience-based?
  • Automation: What level of automation exists? Are processes manual, semi-automated, or fully automated?
  • Cybersecurity: How mature is the OT security posture? Is there network segmentation and access control?
  • Organization & Culture: Is there digital literacy? Cross-functional collaboration? Willingness to change?

The assessment produces a Digital Maturity Score (1-5 scale) per dimension, benchmarked against industry peers, with specific gap identification and priority ranking.

Industry 4.0 Roadmap

Based on the maturity assessment and your strategic objectives, we develop a comprehensive 3-year transformation roadmap:

  • Phased Implementation Plan: Year 1 (foundation: connectivity + data platform), Year 2 (intelligence: analytics + AI pilots), Year 3 (optimization: autonomous operations + ecosystem integration).
  • ROI Projection: Detailed financial model with CapEx, OpEx, savings, and payback period for each initiative.
  • Technology Architecture: Reference architecture showing how MES, SCADA, ERP, IoT, and AI layers interact.
  • Risk Register: Identified risks (technical, organizational, vendor) with mitigation strategies.

OT/IT Convergence Strategy

The convergence of Operational Technology (OT) and Information Technology (IT) is both a technical challenge and an organizational one. SURIOTA designs convergence strategies that address both:

  • Network Architecture: Purdue model-aligned network design with secure demilitarized zones (DMZ) between OT and IT networks.
  • Security Framework: IEC 62443-based cybersecurity strategy covering asset inventory, risk assessment, access control, and incident response.
  • Data Governance: Ownership models, data quality standards, master data management, and compliance frameworks.

Our Consulting Framework

We follow a proven 5-phase framework adapted from leading management consulting practices and tailored for industrial digitalization:

  1. Assess: Current state analysis, maturity assessment, stakeholder interviews, and objective setting.
  2. Design: Target state architecture, roadmap development, technology selection, and business case creation.
  3. Pilot: Controlled proof-of-concept on a limited scope to validate assumptions and demonstrate value.
  4. Scale: Phased rollout across the organization with continuous monitoring and adjustment.
  5. Optimize: Continuous improvement based on operational data, feedback loops, and emerging technologies.
