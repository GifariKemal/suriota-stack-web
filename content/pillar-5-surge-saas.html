Platform Modules

SURGE-Energy Mapping

Energy is invisible until you measure it — and most industrial facilities only see their total monthly electricity bill, not where energy is consumed, when peaks occur, or which machines are inefficient. SURGE-Energy Mapping changes this:

  • Real-Time kWh Monitoring: Live energy consumption per machine, production line, department, or facility — updated every second.
  • Cost Allocation: Automatically allocate energy costs to cost centers, products, or customers based on actual consumption.
  • Peak Demand Alerting: Predictive alerts before demand threshold breaches, enabling load shedding or scheduling changes to avoid demand charges.
  • Carbon Footprint Tracking: Automatic CO2 equivalent calculation based on grid emission factors and renewable energy offsets.

Typical Results: 15-25% reduction in energy costs within 12 months through visibility-driven optimization.

SURGE-Vessel Tracking

Maritime and logistics operations face unique visibility challenges. Vessels operate beyond cellular coverage. Fuel theft is difficult to detect. Maintenance schedules are often reactive. SURGE-Vessel Tracking solves these problems:

  • GPS + IoT Sensor Integration: Real-time vessel position, speed, heading, and route history on an interactive map.
  • Fuel Consumption Monitoring: NMEA-2000 or Modbus-connected fuel flow meters with automated theft detection algorithms.
  • Geofence Alerting: Automatic alerts when vessels enter restricted zones, deviate from planned routes, or exceed speed limits.
  • Maintenance Scheduling: Engine hours-based maintenance reminders with parts ordering integration.

Connectivity: Coastal 4G LTE with automatic Iridium/Inmarsat satellite failover for offshore operations. Never lose tracking coverage.

SURGE-Water Analytics

Water treatment compliance is not optional — and the penalties for non-compliance can shut down operations. SURGE-Water Analytics ensures you stay ahead of regulatory requirements:

  • KLHK SPARING Compliance Dashboard: Native integration with Indonesia's KLHK SPARING system per Permen LHK No. 80/2019. Automated data submission for pH, COD, TSS, NH3-N, and flow parameters.
  • Real-Time Parameter Monitoring: pH, COD, BOD, TSS, ammonia, turbidity, conductivity, dissolved oxygen, and flow — all on one dashboard.
  • Automatic Report Generation: Daily, weekly, and monthly compliance reports formatted for regulatory submission.
  • Regulatory Submission Ready: One-click export of compliance data in formats accepted by KLHK and regional environmental agencies.

Wastewater Logger

  • IPAL Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of industrial wastewater treatment plant performance.
  • Flow & Quality Data Logging: Tamper-proof data logging with timestamps for audit trails.
  • KLHK Integration: Direct data transmission to KLHK servers for industries under mandatory monitoring requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deployment take?

Single-site pilots can be live within 1-2 weeks. Enterprise multi-site rollouts typically take 4-8 weeks depending on network infrastructure and sensor installation requirements.

Can we try before buying?

Yes. We offer a 30-day free pilot on up to 10 devices with full platform access. No credit card required. No obligation to continue.

What happens if we exceed our device limit?

The platform scales automatically. Additional devices are billed pro-rata at your plan's per-device rate. We never throttle your data or restrict functionality.

Is our data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). For on-premise deployments, your data never leaves your network. We conduct annual penetration testing and maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance.

Can you integrate with our existing SCADA?

Yes. SURGE can ingest data via OPC UA, Modbus, MQTT, or database connectors from your existing SCADA or DCS. Many clients use SURGE as a cloud-based layer above their on-premise SCADA for remote access and analytics.
