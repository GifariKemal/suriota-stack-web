Electrical Engineering

Electrical infrastructure is the lifeblood of any industrial facility. SURIOTA's electrical engineering team designs, installs, and maintains power systems that are safe, compliant, and optimized for your operational needs.

Panel Installation & Fabrication

  • MCC (Motor Control Centers): Design and fabrication of MCCs with variable frequency drives (VFDs), soft starters, and direct-on-line starters.
  • PCC (Power Control Centers): Low-voltage switchgear with ACB, MCCB, and protection relay coordination.
  • Distribution Panels: Power distribution boards with proper busbar sizing, short-circuit calculations, and selective coordination.
  • Custom Control Panels: Purpose-built panels for specific machinery or process skids with integrated PLCs, HMIs, and instrumentation.

Power Distribution Systems

  • Transformer Sizing & Installation: Load flow analysis, transformer selection, installation, and commissioning.
  • Cable Routing & Termination: Cable tray design, cable sizing per IEC 60364, glanding, and termination with proper torque specifications.
  • Load Balancing: Three-phase load analysis and rebalancing to minimize neutral current and voltage imbalance.

Compliance & Standards

  • SNI Compliance: All electrical works comply with Indonesian National Standards (SNI).
  • IEC & PUIL 2011: International Electrotechnical Commission standards and Indonesian electrical installation regulations.
  • Insulation Resistance Testing: Megger testing of cables and motors per IEC 61543.
  • Thermography Inspection: Infrared thermal scanning of electrical panels and connections to identify hot spots before failure.

IoT-Ready Integration

  • Smart Metering: Installation of PM1611-WD and similar smart power meters with Modbus/IoT connectivity.
  • Energy Monitoring: Real-time kWh monitoring per machine, line, or department for cost allocation and efficiency tracking.
  • Power Quality Analysis: Harmonic analysis, power factor measurement, and voltage sag/swell recording.

Safety-First Workflow

  • LOTO (Lockout/Tagout) Procedures: Documented energy isolation procedures for all electrical maintenance activities.
  • Arc Flash Study: Arc flash hazard analysis per IEEE 1584 with proper PPE categorization and labeling.
  • Grounding System Design: Earth grid design, soil resistivity testing, and grounding system commissioning per IEEE 80.
